AI-ISCO: Job Evolution Explorer GitHub

An interactive visualization of ESCO occupations mapped to ISCO-08, scored by AI along two independent axes: Automation Risk (how much AI can replace tasks) and Amplification Potential (how much AI can enhance the worker). These combine into an Evolution Potential score that captures the net direction of change. Each rectangle's area is proportional to the number of skills in that occupation. Color reflects the selected scoring dimension.

The 2x2 Quadrant Model: By crossing automation risk and amplification potential, every occupation falls into one of four quadrants:

TRANSFORM — High auto + high amp. Job evolves into something new and better.
SHRINK — High auto + low amp. Job at risk of decline or displacement.
EVOLVE — Low auto + high amp. Job enhanced and empowered by AI.
STABLE — Low auto + low amp. Job largely unaffected by AI.

Caveat: All scores are LLM-generated estimates based on ESCO skill profiles, not rigorous economic forecasts. A high automation risk does not mean the job will disappear — it may transform, grow, or be protected by regulation, demand elasticity, or social preference. Use this as an exploration tool, not a prediction engine.
